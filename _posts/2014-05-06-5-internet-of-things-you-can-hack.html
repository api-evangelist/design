There may well be 50 billion device coming, but the most exciting things in the Internet of Things are the ones you can hack.I’ve developed a new weekend hobby of connecting and hacking devices.Here are my top 5: Philips Hue These connected lights bulbs have an HTTP API that is really easy to use and allows you to control single and groups of lights.You can control the colour range, brights and hue of course. Furthermore your partner will love the pretty colours and you’ll convince your kids you can do magic.
